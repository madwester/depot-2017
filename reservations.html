Bookings

We are happy to take bookings on weekdays. On weekends we take bookings for groups of 10 or more. If you would like to make a booking please call ahead. Please arrive on time as unfortunately we can only hold a table for 10 minutes before it is given to the next group in the queue.

Most weekend mornings we have a wait list, so for groups smaller than 10, we recommend calling 15-20 minutes ahead to be added to the list. Otherwise send a friend from your party down early and we can add you to the wait list. We will do our very best to accommodate you!

Functions

The Depot offers both onsite and offsite catering for functions, parties and events. You might like to consider us for your next dinner party, birthday, staff party, engagement party or any sort of celebration!

Our venue is available to hire exclusively for stand up cocktail events or sit down dinners and we will design a menu and drinks package to suit your requirements and budget. Please send us an email with your function or catering request here.
